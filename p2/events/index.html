Pines of Nowhere

Pines of Nowhere hosts special events in the restaurant. The restaurant allows full buy outs for events but also will allow events hosted on the patio and in the private dining room.

We are excited to offer and host events for our community. With our extensive restaurant, events may be hosted on the patio and in private dining rooms for your benefit.

You can sit comfortably and catch up with friends while you have an experience of a lifetime. Free high speed wifi is always available.

Just see the barista to reserve and plan your event (Business Meetings/Lunch, Parties, Charity Events, Baby/Bridal Shower, etc).

We also provide events throughout the week for kids and adults of all ages, we are blessed to offer game nights/afternoons and live music.

Our menus are also customizable for your event to make your experience a good one. We hope you feel at home here!
